Bio

Provost of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, holds multiple portfolios such as Strategic Initiatives, Research & Innovation, Interdisciplinary and AI+X. She is the Dean of International Programs and School for Sustainable Futures, and the Director & Professor at Amrita Center for Wireless Networks and Applications (AWNA).

Dr. Maneesha Vinodini Ramesh pursued her Ph. D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and authored a research thesis titled Wireless Sensor Network to Detect Rainfall Induced Landslides. Her research work was instrumental in deploying the first ever Wireless Sensor Network system capable of issuing landslide warning. She has received U.S. Patent US 13/168,3572014 for this work, "Network based system for predicting landslides and providing early warnings".
